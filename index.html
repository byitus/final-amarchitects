Studio // 01

Designed with context.
Built with care.

AM Architects is led by Principal Architect Akibha Muneesh, carrying every commission from first sketch through interior detailing, landscape and construction.

"Architecture is more than just designing buildings — it's about shaping experiences, enhancing lifestyles, and creating spaces that stand the test of time."

Founded with a vision to deliver thoughtful, refined and functional design, the studio specialises in residential, commercial and interior architecture. Every project is approached with a close reading of context, purpose and the unique needs of each client — from a single-family residence to a multi-storey hospital.

The practice carries projects in-house across four disciplines, so the building handed over on site is the one imagined at the desk: architecture, interior design, construction and landscape, under one studio.

Four disciplines,
one studio.

Architecture, interior design, construction and landscape — carried in-house, end to end, so nothing is lost in translation between disciplines.
